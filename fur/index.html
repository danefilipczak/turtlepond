This is a variation by Young ('84) of the reaction / diffusion originally introduced by Turing ('52). This model recasts the inhibitor / activator paradigm as a cellular automata, with each cell having an inner and outer elliptical neighborhood. "On" cells in a given cell's inner neighborhood work as activators while "on" cells in the outer neighborhood work as inhibitors.

D. A. Young. A local activator-inhibitor model of vertebrate skin patterns. Math. Biosciences, 72:51-58, 1984.
